Case Study

Proven mystery shopping track record across UAE retail and QSR sectors — directly relevant to the Jollibee engagement.

🏪 Hypermarkets — Cooperative Society · UAE
Retail Compliance Mission
Mystery Shopping · Consumer Protection Standards
Client Challenge
Cooperative societies needed to meet UAE standards across Safety, Cleanliness, Promotions, Pricing, Product Knowledge and Consumer Data Protection — ensuring all transactions met transparency and complaint management standards.
MBG Approach
Deployed trained professionals posing as regular customers. Developed detailed evaluation checklists based on best practices. Shoppers conducted interactions across various scenarios, recording compliance observations focused on UAE regulatory standards.
Key Outcomes
Clear understanding of compliance levels with consumer protection standards
Specific service gaps identified affecting customer experience
Strengthened internal controls & risk management frameworks
Long-term operational resilience supported
🍔 Quick Service Restaurants · Multi-Branch UAE
QSR Service Excellence Mission
Mystery Shopping · Product Knowledge & Service Quality
Client Challenge
Client sought to ensure employees were well-trained in product knowledge and exceptional service. Assessed whether customers consistently received clear, accurate product information and whether interactions met high standards of professionalism.
MBG Approach
Deployed mystery shoppers briefed on specific product knowledge scenarios. Developed a customised evaluation framework measuring product knowledge, communication clarity, professionalism, responsiveness and ethical cross-selling across multiple branches.
Key Outcomes
Knowledge gaps revealed among frontline staff in key product areas
Unclear communication and T&C explanation instances identified
Responsiveness & professionalism gaps highlighted
Proactive staff training programme enabled
